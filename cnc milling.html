What is CNC Milling?

CNC milling is a manufacturing process that uses computer-controlled machines to remove material from a workpiece.
The process involves the use of a rotating cutting tool to cut the desired shape from a solid block of material.

The milling machines are highly precise and can produce complex shapes. They can also be programmed to produce multiple identical parts, making the milling process a cost-effective solution for production.
